Carbonara

Description

This is a recipe for Carbonara.

Carbonara is an Italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs yolks
  • Some spaghetti
  • Parmesan
  • Pancetta

Steps

  1. Put the Pancetta in a pan with no oil
  2. Cook until done
  3. Cook spaghetti
  4. Add speghetti to pan with a little pasta water
  5. Mix the eggs and parmesan
  6. Take pan off heat and leave to cool for a couple of minutes
  7. Add the Pamersan mix
